Class Restaurant

//Attributes
isOpen (boolean)
location (string)
menu (array)
employees (array)
//Methods
openRestaurant (updates the isOpen attribute to true)
closeRestaurant (updates the isOpen attribute to false)
addMenuItem (updates the menu attribute by adding one new item)
removeMenuItem (updates the menu attribute by removing one old item
hireEmployee (updates the employees attribute by adding one new employee)
fireEmployee (updates the employees attribute by removing one old employee)
